# 🚀 The Business Solutions Series
### Empowering the Unplugged Shopkeeper with Intelligence Systems

Welcome to the **Business Solutions Series**. This repository is not just a collection of files; it is a portfolio of **problem-solving engines** designed for small, non-digital businesses in high-inflation, low-tech environments (specifically the Pakistani market).

## 🎯 The Mission
Traditional ERP software is often too expensive, too complex, or requires a PC that small shopkeepers don't have. My mission is to build **High-Logic, Low-Cost** systems using Excel, Power Query, and AI to solve "bleeding-neck" business problems.

## 🛠️ The Philosophy
- **Problem-First:** Every project starts with a real-world interview with a shopkeeper.
- **Actionable Intelligence:** Moving beyond "tracking data" to "suggesting decisions" (e.g., *Return this stock now* or *Your cheque will bounce*).
